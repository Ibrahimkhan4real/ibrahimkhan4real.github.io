about

I'm an AI researcher from Pakistan, now living in United Kingdom. I have a passion for coding and love to experiment with things. While I work as a PhD, the opinions expressed on this blog are solely my own and do not reflect the University's policy in any way. Most of my work is only available within Anthropic or Google, but see publications for what is public.

These days, I mostly write C++ and Python with a sprinkling of TypeScript for work, with a very long tail of other languages. Over time I ended up with readability in 6.

For my personal projects, I prefer Rust over C++, but Python and TypeScript are just as handy as at work.

As for the languages I speak, check out my post on language learning.
