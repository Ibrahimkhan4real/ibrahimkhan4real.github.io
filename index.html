About

My research develops tree-search methods that let controllers plan ahead under uncertainty. The core idea is to use MCTS as a model-predictive controller for heating systems: the algorithm simulates thousands of possible futures, then picks the action sequence that minimises energy consumption while keeping occupant comfort within bounds.
