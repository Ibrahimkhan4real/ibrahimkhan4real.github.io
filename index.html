Flagship project

Autonomous Laser Weeding Robot

I engineered an agricultural robot that autonomously navigates crop rows, detects weeds via computer vision, and eliminates them using a 10W carbon laser. The platform combines:

  • Advanced weed detection based on shape, size, and colour.
  • Laser actuation with CNC precision to remove weeds and confirm success in real time.
  • A novel four-bar-linkage chassis for stability across uneven farmland.
  • Path-planning algorithms that keep the robot fully autonomous.

This system placed third in the Prime Minister’s National Innovation Award 2023 and provides an eco-friendly alternative to chemical herbicides.
