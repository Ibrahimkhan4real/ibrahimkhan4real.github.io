Autonomous Laser Weeding Robot

Project Overview

Developed an innovative agricultural robot designed to autonomously navigate and weed crop fields, utilizing advanced computer vision and laser technology.

Key Features

  • Advanced Weed Detection: implemented a sophisticated computer vision algorithm to distinguish weeds from crops based on shape, size, and color, ensuring precise identification.
  • Laser Elimination: integrated a 10W Carbon laser that targets and eliminates weeds with a five-second exposure time, offering an efficient and eco-friendly alternative to herbicides.
  • Real-Time Verification: used computer vision to confirm weed removal before resuming navigation, guaranteeing thorough results.
  • Novel Chassis Design: engineered a unique chassis based on four-bar linkages, enhancing stability and mobility on farmland.
  • Autonomous Navigation: developed a path-planning algorithm so the robot can navigate crop fields end-to-end.
  • Precision Actuation: installed a CNC mechanism to align the laser precisely above every weed.

Achievements

  • Secured third place in Prime Minister’s National Innovation Award 2023.
  • Delivered a sustainable, environmentally friendly alternative to chemical herbicides.
  • Reduced dependency on chemicals by providing a fully autonomous, real-time verified weeding workflow.
